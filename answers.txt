Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: For collaborative projects, to keep remote backups, to host websites, and to share projects with other people.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves history of the commits that were merged, while rebase removes commits to keep history in a straight line.
